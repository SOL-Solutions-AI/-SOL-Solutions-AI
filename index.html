SOLutions – Prep for VA SOL

SOLutions provides Virginia K–12 students structured, reliable support for Standards of Learning (SOL) preparation, class study, unit tests, and quizzes. The study bot is powered by Playlab and functions like a real educational app.

How It Works

Workflow: Setup → Diagnostics → Review → Mastery → Practice. Each mode addresses specific learning needs and guides students step by step.

Design Principles

Responses stay accurate, concise, and student-focused. No hallucinations, no personal info collection, and missing data is flagged, not fabricated.

Learning Focus

Students can review notes, flashcards, mnemonics, and adaptive practice questions. Navigation is clear: always know where you are and what comes next.
